Agro Mobile App

A platform that connects agriculture startups with investors, as well as providing resources and mentorship for growth. Farmers may use this platform to manage their business, communicate with specialists, and access finance and investment options.

introduction

Problem Statement

Inadequate capital to invest in agriculture. Due to a lack of financial resources, the high cost of contemporary farming equipment and technology, the difficulties in scaling up operations, the inability to earn enough income from farming, and a lack of investment prospects.

My Role

I was the project's single senior UI/UX engineer. UX design, graphic design, branding, and identity were all part of my design responsibilities. User interviews, user personas, user flows, drawing, wire-framing, and prototyping were all part of my deliverables.

Tools

  • Design tools such as Figma to create wireframes, mockups, and prototypes of the application's user interface.
  • Project management tool used is Devops to manage the development process and keep track of tasks and progress.
Problem Discovery

Addressing the Obstacles of Farmers' Inadequate Capital to Invest in Agriculture

An Agriculture app can be introduced that provides financial assistance in form of loans or investments, expertise and mentorship to help with farming operations, access to resources and technology to increase efficiency and ability to connect with potential buyers and markets.

Our Solution

One-stop agricultural app for farmers and investors

  • Crop management

    Help farmers monitor and manage their crops, providing real-time data on factors such as soil moisture, temperature, and pest infestations.

  • Livestock management

    Providing farmers with real-time data on feed consumption and health. This information can help farmers to make more informed decisions about feed rations, veterinary treatments, and breeding programs.

  • Precision agriculture

    Apps can also be used to support precision agriculture practices, helping farmers to optimize their operations through the use of data and technology.

Proccess

Primary Research

I performed interviews to obtain additional information on how effectively the suggested solution should be constructed for the consumers. Through the interviews, I was able to learn about the features and services that users want from a health and fitness app. Additionally, I also conducted an online survey to obtain input and ideas from key user groups. I was able to interview Mr. Yeshan Perera and Ms. Natalie Fernando in order to acquire the necessary information. They are both highly motivated people who are concerned about their health and fitness. Below are some of the questions that were asked during the interview.

  • What type of fitness and health apps have they used?
  • What sought of features are they looking for in a fitness and health app?
  • Have they come across apps that have all in one solution for health and fitness?
  • Issues they have faced when dealing with the apps?
  • What type of app would they consider worth purchasing?
  • How do they select the best health and fitness app for them?

My intended group size for the online questionnaires was 65 people. And I got answers from 50 people.
